# Product-Adapted Scene Assets

## Goal

Create twelve reusable, photorealistic 9:16 location assets for jewelry advertising. Each scene must feel like a single real location where an adult model can stand, sit, lean, or pass through without appearing composited into a background.

## Shared Direction

All scenes use a coherent Mediterranean late-afternoon editorial language: pale limestone, weathered ivory plaster, olive or coastal foliage, physically consistent warm side light, natural contact shadows, environmental bounce light, foreground depth, and an unforced camera viewpoint. No empty studio backdrops, isolated cutout subjects, artificial bokeh, text, logos, cups, tables, laptops, vases, or unrelated product props.

## Scene Set

| Group | Scene IDs | Product use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Earring world | sea-stair landing, limestone arch shade, terrace railing | ear-profile and half-body shots |
| Bracelet world | poolside lounger edge, water-side stone ledge, olive-tree bench | wrist foreground and seated lifestyle shots |
| Necklace world | rooftop doorway, sunset terrace, linen window chair | collarbone and half-body shots |
| Universal transitions | moving white-wall shade, planted courtyard passage, sea-view corridor | opening hooks, endings, and cross-product transitions |

## Per-Scene Deliverable

Each scene has one clean vertical location image designed as a usable base frame, with:

- a defined model position or movement lane;
- foreground, middle distance, and background depth;
- a single stated natural light direction;
- a product-safe framing zone for earrings, bracelet wrists, or necklaces;
- a Chinese four-second I2V prompt that permits only one environmental motion and one camera motion.

## Relationships

Each scene is stored under `03-scenes` using a stable `SCENE_FOUNDATION_*` ID, linked to `STYLE_FOUNDATION_MEDITERRANEAN_EDITORIAL_001`, and represented by an Obsidian asset card. Product use is tagged rather than locked to one SKU so future products can reuse the location.

## Acceptance

An accepted image must be vertical 9:16, contain no visible cutout edge or staged isolated-subject look, preserve plausible scale and perspective for a model, and make the intended product framing zone usable. Scene cards must expose the image, scene purpose, compatible product types, model position, light direction, and copyable I2V prompt.

## Out Of Scope

This batch creates location assets only. It does not add a model, a product, generated video, storefront content, or standalone decorative still-life objects.
