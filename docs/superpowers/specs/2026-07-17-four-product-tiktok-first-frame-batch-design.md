# Four-Product TikTok First-Frame Batch Design

## Objective

Create a cold-start social advertising package for four New Series products. Each product receives four photorealistic 9:16 first frames designed for 4-second Seedance 2.0 Fast I2V clips, plus a compact TikTok distribution package and an Obsidian shot card.

The work belongs in the Obsidian film asset library. It must not replace or publish storefront product imagery.

Deliver 16 first frames in total. Every frame is animated; no static filler clip is planned.

## Shared Art Direction

- Photorealistic editorial jewelry photography with natural skin texture, believable pores, fine hair, fabric weave, and physically plausible light.
- Mediterranean coastal architecture, limestone, olive foliage, sea light, restrained warm wood, and real atmospheric depth.
- Use the established MythRealms model and environment references only as style anchors. Do not copy jewelry from those references.
- Preserve the selected product's exact silhouette, material arrangement, color placement, scale, clasp or hook geometry, and number of visible components.
- No generated words, logos, watermarks, packaging, extra jewelry, unrelated tabletop props, duplicated products, or invented gemstones.
- Each 4-second clip contains one subject action and one controlled camera move. Product readability wins over camera spectacle.
- Final copy and music are added in post, never generated inside the Seedance clip.

## Product 004: The Violet Rain

**Visual world:** blue-hour limestone courtyard with restrained violet reflections and warm practical spill.

**Model:** realistic dark-haired woman with olive skin, natural makeup, black or deep plum sleeveless evening top.

| Frame | Visual contract | 4-second motion contract | End state |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 01 Macro hook | One earring fills the upper-middle frame; irregular central white pearl and purple stone border are crisp. | Slow 85 mm lateral micro-slide while one violet highlight travels across the stone border. | Pearl remains sharp; highlight exits frame right. |
| 02 Pair proof | Exact pair rests on dark wet limestone, separated enough to read both silhouettes. | Slow overhead arc; a narrow reflected light passes once across both earrings. | Both earrings remain fully visible and unchanged. |
| 03 Wearing proof | Tight side profile with one earring unobstructed; ear, jawline, and shoulder form a clean diagonal. | Model turns her chin about 10 degrees toward the practical light; camera makes a minimal push-in. | Earring stops at its brightest readable angle. |
| 04 Distinct ending | Medium-wide view through a courtyard arch; model crosses into the right third and looks back once. | Camera tracks sideways while the model takes two measured steps and turns her head. | Face and earring hold on the right; arch and evening sky provide left-side negative space. |

TikTok hook: `Pearls don't have to whisper.`

BGM direction: TikTok Commercial Music Library, nocturnal downtempo instrumental, 96-100 BPM, dry percussion, muted bass, no vocals.

## TikTok Package Per Product

Each shot card includes:

- One traffic-first English caption, one engagement caption, and one price-led caption.
- One pinned comment that sends users directly to the exact product through the bio link.
- Five focused hashtags, including `#MythRealms`.
- Organic and paid UTM URLs using the exact product slug.
- One recommended CML music search recipe and two fallback directions.
- A 10-12 second edit plan compiled from the four 4-second source clips.
- A 24-hour test rule that changes only one variable at a time.

## Generation Order And Review Gates

Generate product by product in the order 004, 008, 012, 016. For each product:

1. Generate the four first frames using the product gallery as the identity reference and the approved realistic model/environment library as the style reference.
2. Review product geometry, duplicated elements, hands, ears, neckline, and action-ready composition.
3. Repair only failed frames before moving to the next product.
4. Write the Seedance prompts and TikTok package after the four images pass visual review.

This order limits style drift and prevents a geometry error from propagating across a full batch.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Sixteen portrait images exist and decode successfully at an approximately 9:16 ratio.
- All four products have four visually distinct compositions.
- The advertised product is recognizable against its source gallery; no other jewelry is introduced.
- All frames are practical starting states for a single 4-second motion.
- Every frame has a Chinese Seedance I2V prompt with one camera move, one subject action, product locks, and an explicit end state.
- Every product has valid organic and paid product-page URLs with distinct UTM parameters.
- All local image links in the four Obsidian cards resolve.
- The 5174 asset preview displays all four shot cards and opens every first frame.
