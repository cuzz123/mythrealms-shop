# MythRealms Social Media Strategy

## Pinterest Strategy

### Why Pinterest
- Jewelry is the #2 category on Pinterest after home decor
- 85% of Pinners use the platform to plan purchases
- Pins have a 4-month lifespan (vs 24hr for Instagram posts)
- 60%+ of Pinterest users are women 25-45 (our exact target)

### Pin Types (Create 5 per product = 365 pins for 73 products)

**Pin 1: Product Hero Shot**
- Image: Luxury product photo from our collection
- Title: Product name with benefit
- Description: 1-2 sentence mythic story + material + price range
- Board: "Mythical Jewelry Collection"

**Pin 2: Myth Story Card**
- Image: Canva-designed card with beast illustration on left + story snippet
- Title: "The Legend Behind This Piece"
- Description: 2-sentence origin myth from Shan Hai Jing
- Board: "Chinese Mythology & Legends"

**Pin 3: Zodiac/Personality Match**
- Image: "Which Guardian Matches Your Spirit?" text overlay on product shot
- Title: Personality quiz hook
- Description: Link to Guardian Quiz page
- Board: "Find Your Guardian"

**Pin 4: Close-up Detail**
- Image: Macro shot of gemstone/texture/engraving
- Title: Material + craft highlight
- Description: Craftsmanship detail
- Board: "Luxury Jewelry Details"

**Pin 5: Lifestyle/Inspiration**
- Image: Product on marble with soft styling props (candle, silk, ancient book)
- Title: Vibe/aesthetic description
- Description: "For the one who..." emotional hook
- Board: "Modern Mythical Style"

### Posting Schedule
- 5 new Pins per day (1 product fully covered)
- Use Tailwind or Later.com for scheduling
- Best times: 8pm-11pm EST, weekends
- Create 10 boards themed around mythology, jewelry, gemstones, fashion

## TikTok Strategy

### Content Types (3 videos per week)

**Format 1: Guardian Quiz Results (30 sec)**
- Screen recording of taking the quiz
- Dramatic reveal of result
- Text overlay: "Which Chinese guardian are YOU?"
- Link in bio to quiz page

**Format 2: Product Unboxing ASMR (15-30 sec)**
- Close-up of packaging, unwrapping
- No talking, soft ancient/mythical music
- Text: product name + material + price
- Satisfying, luxurious feel

**Format 3: Myth Story (60 sec)**
- "Did you know the Nine-Tailed Fox was originally a GOOD omen?"
- Quick myth busting with text overlay
- Product cameo at the end
- Educational + entertaining

## Meta Ads Strategy (Month 2+)

### Campaign Structure
1. **Top of Funnel (Interest)** — $10/day
   - Interests: Chinese mythology, fantasy jewelry, spiritual jewelry, Wicca, crystals, astrology, tarot
   - Ad: Guardian Quiz → "Which mythical guardian protects you?"
   
2. **Middle of Funnel (Consideration)** — $10/day
   - Retargeting: Viewed product, viewed collection
   - Ad: Best-selling product carousel

3. **Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)** — $10/day
   - Retargeting: Added to cart, initiated checkout
   - Ad: MYTH15 discount code + urgency

- Quiz → /guardian-quiz
- Product ads → specific product pages
- Collection ads → collection pages
